Create your own colour palette

Colours have a powerful effect on us, they can lift your mood or bring back memories.

In this activity you will learn how to use photos to select colours to create a particular mood. These colours can then create a palette that can be used to decorate a room, help you choose clothes or make artworks that express your unique personality.

This course was created by - Fresh Arts
Visit their website: www.nbt.nhs.uk/fresh-arts
